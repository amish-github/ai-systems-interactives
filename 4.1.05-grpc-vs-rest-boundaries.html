Boundary 02
Order → Inventory sync on a hot path
caller: internal service traffic: ~80,000 calls/min

Your Order service must check stock availability on every purchase attempt. Profiling shows this internal call fires 80,000 times a minute and the JSON round-trips are adding measurable latency. Both services are owned by your team and share the same build pipeline.

Boundary 03
Continuous telemetry stream from edge nodes
caller: internal service pattern: continuous stream

A fleet of edge inference nodes must push sensor readings to a central aggregator service in real time — not as individual request-response pairs, but as an ongoing flow of messages. Both endpoints are internal services you control and own.

Boundary 04
Internal admin report export
caller: internal ops team traffic: ~50 calls/day

Your operations team triggers a bulk export report once or twice a day from an internal dashboard. The call is infrequent, performance is not a concern, and the team uses a simple curl script to kick off the job. No streaming needed.
