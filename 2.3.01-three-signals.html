The three signals, in one sentence each
Logs — structured records of individual events; query them to understand exactly what happened in one specific case.
Metrics — numbers sampled over time; use them for dashboards, alerts, and an aggregate view of system health.
Traces — timed spans stitched across services; use them to find which hop in a multi-service request consumed the time.
